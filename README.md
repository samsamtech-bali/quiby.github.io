# Privacy Policy for Quiby

**Last updated: November 24, 2024**

## INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### Personal Information:
• Username you provide during registration
• Smoking cessation data (quit date, cigarettes per day, price per cigarette)
• Progress tracking data (streak days, money saved, relapse dates)

### Usage Data:
• App usage patterns and feature interactions
• Badge achievements and progress milestones

## HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:
• Provide smoking cessation tracking and progress monitoring
• Enable buddy system features for peer support
• Calculate and display your achievements and badges
• Send push notifications for motivation and reminders

## DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

• Your data is stored securely using Apple's CloudKit service
• Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
• We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties
• Access to your data is restricted to essential app functionality only

## BUDDY SYSTEM

• When you connect with a buddy, you share basic progress information
• Shared data includes streak days and money saved (no personal details)
• You control who you connect with using unique buddy codes

## PUSH NOTIFICATIONS

• We may send motivational notifications and progress updates
• You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time

## CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
